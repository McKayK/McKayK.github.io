    Moment Rings

    Moment Rings was my first Full Stack Development project. I utilized -

    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript
    • Node JS
    • Axios
    • Sequelize
    • Twilio
    • THREE JS

    My brother started a custom ring business and I decided to make him a website that would allow his customers to create a custom ring online and view any changes they made to it in a realtime 3d object. They could pan around the ring, zoom in, and update what the ring looked like. They could then submit their order and it would send me a text message with all of their order information. It was a really fun project and I learned a lot of skills in the process!
